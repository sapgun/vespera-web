VESPERA Pre-v1.0 · Local-first

Vision Enhanced Sovereign Personal Execution & Resource Architecture

Models think. Agents act. You keep continuity and control.

VESPERA is a local-first AI operations control plane for people working across multiple models, agents, creative tools, and automation systems — without letting any single provider own your project memory.

Product goal

Return to any project, understand its state, continue with the right tool, recover important outputs, and retain final authority over consequential actions.

Capture intent → restore project context → route the work → collect the result → govern important changes → preserve continuity

What VESPERA enables

  • Project continuity — objectives, decisions, and outputs stay connected
  • Worker independence — AI systems as replaceable workers, not owners
  • Output recovery — generated artifacts under your lifecycle
  • Human authority — consequential actions require explicit control
  • Audit and recoverability — understandable history and restorable local state

Principles

Local-first by defaultCore workflows must not require mandatory cloud infrastructure.
User-owned canonical stateImportant knowledge and assets remain portable and readable.
Workers, not ownersModels and agents are interchangeable participants.
Human authorityConsequential execution cannot silently bypass the user.

Product boundary

VESPERA coordinates specialist systems — coding agents, local models, creative pipelines, workflow automation, and knowledge environments. It is not a foundation model, full IDE, payment engine, or universal SaaS platform.

Detailed orchestration, policy internals, and advanced routing intelligence are intentionally outside this public overview.

Status

VESPERA is in active pre-v1.0 development: a cross-platform desktop command center with governed local Core, workspace onboarding, project continuity, and task capture. Public engineering details are not published on this site.
